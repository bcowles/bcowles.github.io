Media Consumption Diary

Be more than what you watch.

Mission: To educate people on their media consumption and how it can affect their outlook.

History: Inspired by the reading diaries of Nick Hornby, I started cataloging what i was watching, listening, reading, playing, etc. What I thought were fun distractions took up lots of time and shaped my thinking.

Content tracking:

  • TV
  • Books and magazines
  • Music
  • Podcasts
  • Movies
  • Games

Services will teach people how to track and analyze their content. Time spent is as important as what was consumed.

Favorites will come to the surface. See what leads to procrastination. Understand what's taking root in your brain.
